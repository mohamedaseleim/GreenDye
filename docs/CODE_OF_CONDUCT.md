# Code of Conduct

## GreenDye Academy - Community Code of Conduct

**Last Updated:** November 2, 2025

## Our Pledge

GreenDye Academy is committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free learning environment for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our Standards

### Positive Behavior

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- **Respectful Communication**: Using welcoming and inclusive language
- **Constructive Feedback**: Providing helpful, constructive criticism and gracefully accepting it
- **Empathy**: Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- **Collaboration**: Working together and supporting fellow learners
- **Professionalism**: Maintaining professional conduct in all interactions
- **Acknowledgment**: Giving credit where credit is due
- **Growth Mindset**: Focusing on learning and improvement

### Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are considered unacceptable:

- **Harassment**: Including offensive comments, intimidation, stalking, or unwanted attention
- **Discrimination**: Based on any protected characteristic
- **Threats**: Violent language, threats of violence, or encouraging violence
- **Inappropriate Content**: Sexually explicit or violent material
- **Privacy Violations**: Sharing others' private information without permission
- **Spam**: Unsolicited advertising or promotion
- **Cheating**: Academic dishonesty, plagiarism, or sharing answers
- **Trolling**: Deliberate provocation, insults, or inflammatory comments
- **Impersonation**: Pretending to be someone else
- **Disruptive Behavior**: Interrupting discussions or live sessions

## Learning Environment Guidelines

### In Courses and Forums

- Stay on topic and relevant to the course material
- Ask questions respectfully and with proper context
- Search for existing answers before posting duplicate questions
- Mark solutions when your question is answered
- Share knowledge and help other learners
- Respect instructors' time and expertise
- Provide constructive feedback on courses

### In Live Sessions

- Be punctual and prepared
- Mute your microphone when not speaking
- Use the raise hand feature before speaking
- Avoid background noise and distractions
- Dress appropriately for video sessions
- Stay focused on the session content
- Respect the instructor's authority to manage the session

### In Discussion Forums

- Use clear, descriptive titles for posts
- Format code properly using code blocks
- Include relevant details when asking for help
- Avoid cross-posting the same question
- Thank those who help you
- Update your posts if you find a solution
- Report inappropriate content

## Academic Integrity

### Expected Conduct

- Complete your own assignments and assessments
- Do not share or solicit answers to quizzes or exams
- Properly cite sources and give credit
- Do not upload or distribute copyrighted course materials
- Respect intellectual property rights
- Use course content only for personal learning

### Consequences of Violations

- First offense: Warning and education
- Second offense: Temporary suspension
- Third offense: Permanent ban and certificate revocation
- Severe cases: Immediate account termination

## Privacy and Data Protection

### Respect for Privacy

- Do not share others' personal information
- Obtain permission before recording or screenshotting
- Keep private conversations private
- Respect data protection regulations
- Report privacy breaches to platform administrators

### Your Privacy Rights

- You have the right to control your personal data
- You can adjust privacy settings in your profile
- Review our Privacy Policy for detailed information

## Reporting Guidelines

### How to Report Violations

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

1. **In-Platform Reporting**
   - Use the "Report" button on posts, comments, or profiles
   - Provide specific details and evidence

2. **Email Support**
   - Email: conduct@greendye-academy.com
   - Include: dates, screenshots, and detailed description

3. **Emergency Situations**
   - For immediate safety concerns: emergency@greendye-academy.com
   - Contact local authorities if necessary

### What to Include in Reports

- Your contact information
- Description of the incident
- Date and time of occurrence
- Names or usernames involved
- Screenshots or evidence (if available)
- Any witnesses
- Previous related incidents (if applicable)

## Investigation and Enforcement

### Our Process

1. **Receipt**: We acknowledge all reports within 24 hours
2. **Investigation**: We review the report and gather information
3. **Decision**: We determine appropriate action
4. **Communication**: We inform involved parties of the outcome
5. **Appeal**: Decisions may be appealed within 14 days

### Confidentiality

- All reports are handled confidentially
- Information is shared only on a need-to-know basis
- Identities of reporters are protected
- False reports will result in consequences

## Enforcement Actions

Depending on the severity and frequency of violations:

### Warning
- First-time minor infractions
- Education on proper conduct
- Documentation in user record

### Temporary Suspension
- Repeated minor violations
- Single moderate violation
- Duration: 7-30 days
- Loss of access to platform

### Permanent Ban
- Severe violations
- Repeated moderate violations after suspension
- Complete account termination
- No refund of course fees
- Certificate revocation

### Legal Action
- Criminal behavior
- Severe harassment or threats
- Copyright infringement
- Fraud or identity theft

## Instructor Responsibilities

Instructors and trainers are expected to:
- Model positive behavior
- Create inclusive learning environments
- Respond to questions professionally
- Provide timely and constructive feedback
- Report violations they observe
- Maintain professional boundaries
- Respect student privacy

## Platform Administrator Responsibilities

Administrators commit to:
- Enforce this Code of Conduct fairly and consistently
- Investigate reports promptly and thoroughly
- Protect the privacy of reporters and subjects
- Maintain transparency in decision-making
- Continuously improve platform safety
- Provide education on conduct expectations

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- All platform spaces (courses, forums, chat, messages)
- Official GreenDye Academy social media
- Email communications related to the platform
- Live events and webinars
- Any space representing GreenDye Academy
