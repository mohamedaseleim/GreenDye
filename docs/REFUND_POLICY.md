# Refund Policy

## GreenDye Academy - Refund Policy

**Last Updated:** November 2, 2025

At GreenDye Academy, we strive to provide high-quality educational content. This Refund Policy outlines the terms and conditions under which refunds may be requested and processed.

## 1. Refund Eligibility

### 1.1 Standard Refund Period
- Refunds may be requested within **14 days** of purchase
- The course must not have been completed more than 30% of the content
- No certificate should have been issued for the course

### 1.2 Eligible Courses
- All paid courses are eligible for refunds within the specified period
- Subscription-based access follows different terms (see section 3)
- Free courses are not eligible for refunds

## 2. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will NOT be granted in the following cases:
- Course completion exceeds 30% of content
- More than 14 days have passed since purchase
- Certificate has been issued and downloaded
- Course content has been downloaded or copied
- Violation of Terms of Service
- Fraudulent or abusive refund requests
- Bundle purchases (partial refunds not available)

## 3. Subscription Plans

### 3.1 Monthly Subscriptions
- Can be canceled at any time
- No refunds for partial months
- Access continues until the end of the billing period

### 3.2 Annual Subscriptions
- Refund available within 30 days if less than 10% of content accessed
- Prorated refunds not available after 30 days
- Access continues until the end of the billing period after cancellation

## 4. How to Request a Refund

### 4.1 Refund Request Process
1. Log in to your GreenDye Academy account
2. Navigate to "My Purchases" or "Billing"
3. Click "Request Refund" next to the eligible course
4. Provide a reason for the refund request (optional)
5. Submit the request

### 4.2 Alternative Method
Email our support team at: **refunds@greendye-academy.com** with:
- Your account email
- Order number
- Course name
- Reason for refund (optional)

## 5. Refund Processing

### 5.1 Processing Time
- Refund requests are reviewed within **2-3 business days**
- Approved refunds are processed within **5-10 business days**
- The refund will be credited to your original payment method

### 5.2 Processing Fees
- Payment processing fees are non-refundable
- Currency conversion fees (if applicable) are non-refundable

## 6. Technical Issues

### 6.1 Platform Technical Problems
If you experience technical issues that prevent course access:
- Contact support immediately at **support@greendye-academy.com**
- We will work to resolve the issue within 48 hours
- Refunds may be granted if the issue cannot be resolved

### 6.2 Content Quality Issues
If course content does not match the description:
- Report the issue within 7 days of purchase
- Provide specific details about the discrepancy
- We will review and may offer a refund or course replacement

## 7. Special Circumstances

### 7.1 Exceptional Cases
Refund requests outside the standard policy may be considered for:
- Medical emergencies (documentation required)
- Course cancellation by instructor
- Major platform outages affecting course access
- Duplicate purchases (automatic refund)

### 7.2 Corporate/Bulk Purchases
- Corporate accounts have separate refund terms
- Contact our corporate team at **corporate@greendye-academy.com**

## 8. Chargebacks and Disputes

### 8.1 Policy on Chargebacks
- Contact us before initiating a chargeback with your bank
- Chargebacks may result in account suspension pending investigation
- We will work with you to resolve disputes directly

### 8.2 Dispute Resolution
- All disputes should first be addressed through our support team
- We aim to resolve all disputes within 10 business days

## 9. Refund Methods

Refunds will be issued using the original payment method:
- **Credit/Debit Card**: 5-10 business days
- **PayPal**: 3-5 business days
- **Fawry**: 7-14 business days (Egypt)
- **Paymob**: 5-10 business days

## 10. Partial Refunds

### 10.1 When Partial Refunds Apply
- Course bundles where only one course is unsatisfactory (case-by-case basis)
- Subscription downgrades (prorated credit, not cash refund)

### 10.2 Partial Refund Calculation
Calculated based on:
- Time since purchase
- Content accessed
- Course completion percentage

## 11. Gift Purchases

- Refunds for gifted courses must be requested by the original purchaser
- Gifted courses follow the same refund policy
- Recipient must not have accessed significant course content

## 12. Account Closure

- Refunds are not provided for active courses when closing an account
- Request refunds before closing your account
- Closed accounts forfeit access to all purchased content

## 13. Regional Variations

Some regions may have specific consumer protection laws that supersede this policy:
- **EU residents**: 14-day cooling-off period applies
- **Australian residents**: Australian Consumer Law applies
- **Other regions**: Local consumer protection laws may apply

- Changes will be posted on this page
- Significant changes will be communicated via email
- Purchases made before policy changes follow the previous policy terms
